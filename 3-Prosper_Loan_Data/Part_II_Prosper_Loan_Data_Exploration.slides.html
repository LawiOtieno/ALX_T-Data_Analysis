Part II - Prosper Loan Data Exploration¶

by Lawrence OTIENO.¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I focused on uses of loan taken and factors determining loans given to borrowers.

Key insights are:

  • Number of borrowers per State.
  • Factors affecting loan given.
  • Uses of loan borrowed.

Dataset Overview¶

After Cleaning process, I had 57298 entries in the dataset with 14 columns.

Features of dataset after cleaning are: Term, LoanStatus, BorrowerAPR, BorrowerRate, LenderYield, ProsperScore, ListingCategory, BorrowerState, Occupation, EmploymentStatus, IsBorrowerHomeowner, IncomeRange, LoanOriginalAmount.

(Visualization 1) - Borrowers per State¶

Most borrowers come from California (CA), New York (NY) and Texas (TX) respectively. Wyoming (WY) state have lowest number of borrowers.

(Visualization 2) - Uses for loan borrowed¶

Most loans that were taken was used for debt consolidation.

(Visualization 3) - How income range affects loaning.¶

The ease of getting loan increases with the increase in IncomeRange, and most of the borrowers earn between USD 50,000 to USD 74,999.

(Visualization 4) - How Employment Status affect loan given.¶

There are more distribution across different loan amounts for the Employed meaning that people who are employed are most likely to get loans of higher amounts.People who are Not employed or Retired or Part-time have more distribution around USD 4000 LoanOriginalAmount. This means they are likely to be given lower amount of loans.

(Visualization 5) - How risk score affect loan given.¶

People with higher risk score get lower amount of loans while people with lower risk score get higher amount of loans.
